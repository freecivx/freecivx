FreecivWorld.net is the 3D version of the strategy game Freeciv. Because Civilization should be free, in 3D!

FreecivWorld.net is a free and open-source empire-building strategy game that lets you shape the history of human civilization. Begin your journey in the Stone Age and guide your tribe through centuries of progress, from humble beginnings to the wonders of the Space Age.

Play directly in your web browser for free! For an immersive experience, press F11 to enter fullscreen mode.

We value your privacy; learn more in our Privacy Policy.
FreecivWorld.net is proudly open source and licensed under the AGPL license.
